Good to know

Frequently asked

How does quoting work — why are no prices listed?

Every garden is different. Prices depend on yard size, the number of trees, metres of pathway and how much work is involved, so we quote per job after a quick free consultation. Send us your details on WhatsApp and we'll come back with a fair price.

Do you remove the refuse and garden waste?

Yes — refuse removal is part of what we do. We leave your space clean and tidy when we're done.

Do you bring your own equipment?

Absolutely. Our team arrives fully equipped for the job, from garden services through to tree felling and irrigation repairs.

Which areas do you cover?

We're based in Weltevreden Park and work throughout Roodepoort and the surrounding West Rand suburbs. If you're nearby and not listed, just ask — we most likely cover you.

Do you work at offices and businesses too?

Yes. We look after both homes and corporate outdoor spaces, including regular scheduled garden services and landscape design.
